# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in cfgable a respectful, harassment-free
experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and receive constructive feedback in good faith.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project and helping users.
- Respect maintainers' time by providing clear context, reproduction steps, and
  actionable suggestions.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, personal attacks, sustained disruption, discriminatory language, and
publication of private information without consent are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict
participation when behavior harms the project community. Enforcement decisions are
made in the interest of keeping the project productive and welcoming.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository owner's
preferred contact channel. Security vulnerabilities should be reported through the
process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), not through public issues.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant in a concise form for this
project.
